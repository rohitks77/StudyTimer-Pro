# Privacy Policy for StudyTimer Pro

**Last Updated:** December 31, 2025

## Introduction

StudyTimer Pro ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What Data We Collect

StudyTimer Pro collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Study Session Data**: Study time, session duration, start/stop timestamps
- **Subject Information**: Subject names, progress percentages, completion status
- **User Notes**: Notes you create for subjects and study planner entries
- **Bookmarks**: Saved bookmarks for study materials
- **Achievement Progress**: Unlocked achievements and milestones
- **Settings and Preferences**: Theme, daily goals, notification preferences, branding customization
- **Study History**: Historical study time and session data for statistics

### How Data is Stored

**All data is stored locally using Chrome's Local Storage API.**

- ✅ Data stays on **your device only**
- ✅ **No data is sent to any external servers**
- ✅ **No data is transmitted over the internet**
- ✅ **No analytics or tracking services** are used
- ✅ **No third-party access** to your data

## Data Usage

The data collected is used solely to:

- Track your study time and progress
- Display statistics and achievements
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Provide study planner functionality
- Show historical study data

**We do not:**

- ❌ Collect personal identifying information (name, email, etc.)
- ❌ Send data to external servers
- ❌ Share data with third parties
- ❌ Use tracking or analytics tools
- ❌ Display advertisements
- ❌ Sell your data

## Permissions Explained

StudyTimer Pro requests the following Chrome permissions:

- **storage**: To save your study data, settings, and preferences locally
- **notifications**: To send you study reminders and achievement notifications
- **alarms**: To schedule daily resets and periodic reminders
- **tabs**: To open your study website when you click buttons

**Note:** We do not access, read, or collect data from websites you visit.

## Data Control and Export

You have full control over your data:

- **Export Data**: You can export all your data as a JSON file from the Settings tab
- **Import Data**: You can import previously exported data
- **Delete Data**: You can reset all data at any time from the Settings tab
- **Uninstall**: Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Your data is protected by your device's security measures
- No data transmission means no risk of interception
- No server-side data breaches are possible

## Third-Party Services

StudyTimer Pro does **not** use any third-party services, including:

- No analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
- No crash reporting services
- No advertising networks
- No data collection platforms

## Children's Privacy

StudyTimer Pro is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no specific concerns regarding children's privacy.
